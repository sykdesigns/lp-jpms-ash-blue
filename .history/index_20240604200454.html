GET TO KNOW THE ASH BLUE SERIES

Formulated with a black-to-gray background color and soft blue tone.

Ideal for use on natural levels 3-7.

Will neutralize or correct unwanted orange dominant pigments.

Use alone or added to your formulation for subtle coolness.

the cooldown

Use the Ab shades to provide soft control of the dominant pigment when lifting.

They’re perfect for guests who want to control unwanted warmth and cool enough to create neutral to cool results that still look natural.

WHY THE LOWERCASE “b”?

The capital “A” in Ash indicates the dominant tone, while the lowercase “b” in blue is a softer, more diminished tone. It’s this combination that offers a nuanced result.
